Hasbro Gaming wonderland launches at Changi Airport’s T3 for June holidays

The departure hall in Changi Airport’s Terminal 3 has been transformed into a Hasbro Gaming ‘wonderland of fun’ to keep fans of all ages entertained during the June holiday period. Running from 26 May to 16 July, the Hall of Games brings to life phygital versions of classic favourites including Monopoly, Hungry Hungry Hippos, Twister, The Game of Life and Guess Who?, complete with a whimsical playground, top photo spots, and Changi-exclusive premiums. From striking a pose with Mr. Monopoly to taking part in a fast-paced on-screen game of Hungry Hungry Hippos, there’s lots of fun to be had. One free Gamer Pass can be redeemed with a minimum spend of S$50 (or S$80 in the supermarket) in a single receipt at participating outlets in the public areas of Terminals 1 to 4 (excluding Jewel Changi Airport). Visitors can redeem an additional free pass with payment by Changi Pay or Mastercard. Alternatively, Gamer Passes can be purchased for S$5 each.
