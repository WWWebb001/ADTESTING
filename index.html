Hasbro Gaming wonderland launches at Changi Airport’s T3 for June holidays

The departure hall in Changi Airport’s Terminal 3 has been transformed into a Hasbro Gaming 'wonderland of Running from 26 May to 16 July, the Hall of Games brings to life phygital versions of classic favorites including Monopoly, Hungry Hungry Hippos, Twister, The Game of Life and Guess Who?, complete with a whimsical playground, top photo spots, and Changi-exclusive premiums. One free Gamer Pass can be redeemed with a minimum spend of S$50 (or S$80 in the supermarket) in a single receipt at participating outlets in the public areas of Terminals 1 to 4 (excluding Jewel Changi Airport). Visitors can redeem an additional free pass with payment by Changi Pay or Mastercard. Alternatively, Gamer Passes can be purchased for S$5 each.
